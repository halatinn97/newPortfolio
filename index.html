About

Who am I?

Certified Full-Stack Web Developer. I have gained valuable expertise through mentorship by Bianca Trif, a distinguished Engineering Manager at Monzo. My portfolio showcases a diverse range of Full-Stack applications and websites crafted using JavaScript, HTML, CSS, React, Angular, TypeScript, and various testing frameworks. Wondering what "Full-Stack" really means? In simple terms, it means I possess the skills to engineer both the front end (the user-facing aspects) and back end (the behind-the-scenes data storage and processing) of a website.

My capabilities extend beyond technical proficiency, as I also hold a BSc degree in Media, Communications & Sociology. This interdisciplinary knowledge empowers me to approach projects from different angles, encompassing user behavior, communication strategies, and social dynamics. Leveraging my research-focused background, I possess analytical and research skills that enable me to comprehend user needs, design exceptional user experiences, and develop websites and apps aligned with my clients' objectives. What sets me apart is my diverse educational background, which affords me a unique perspective in web development. This perspective drives me to create websites that not only excel technically but also resonate with users on a profound level, considering inclusivity, accessibility, and effective communication.

At the core of my work philosophy lies a commitment to delivering high-quality results while actively considering and incorporating your distinct needs and feedback. With every project, my goal is to provide exceptional solutions that go beyond expectations, catering to the broader impact and success of your digital presence.
